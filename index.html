Hi Mark. This is Fuzzy Sweater.

We are a small team of artists in Philadelphia, PA whose goal is to document the areas thriving music and arts scene.

Live performances from the areas up and coming artists

Our goal is to provide full audio & video live performance sessions for local artists and their fans.

Fuzzy Sweater is a production (company?) made up of a couple local artists and their friends, working out of a basement in the hilly Manayunk section of Philadelphia, PA. Prior to Covid-18, the basement operated as a DIY music venue for touring and local bands. As it became unfortunately clear that live music was still a whiles away, Shane & Matt decided to take this as an opportunity to put their audio/video skills to use. Taking notes from bigger names like Audiotree and KEXP-FM, Fuzzy Sweaters approach to recorded live music emphasizes the intimacy and warmth of a live basement show experience.

More About Fuzzy Sweater

Fully Recorded Audio & Video Live Sessions

From cameras and lighting to microphones and cables, we are here to record live music

Lighting & Video

We miss watching live bands perform and are here to try to capture a tiny part of that experience for you. Unfortunately, we can't replicate the 15 minute line for the bathroom, but we can make sure the energy of your favorite local band is captured for your eye balls.

Sound & Audio

Live streams are an okay substitute for a full live performance, but most times, phones just don't cut it. Our goal is to capture the sounds that are coming out of Philly (and really anyone passing through) in the highest quality we can.

Assured Basement Quality

Ask anyone in a band for a great gig story and 9 times out of 10, it takes place in a claustrophobic, West Philly basement (or living room or kitchen.) We don't claim to be DIY aficionados but our hope is that watching a Fuzzy Sweater session is like a breath of sweaty, musty air.
