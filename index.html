We are Fuzzy Sweater.

We're a small team of artists in Philadelphia, PA who want to document the areas thriving music and arts scene.

Check out our latest video!

Live performances from local artists

Our goal is to provide in studio live sessions for artists and their fans.

Fuzzy Sweater is made up of a couple local artists and their friends, working out of a basement in the hilly Manayunk section of Philadelphia, PA. Prior to Covid-19, the basement operated as a DIY music venue for touring and local bands. As it became unfortunately clear that live music was still a far away dream, Shane & Matt decided to take this as an opportunity to put their audio/video skills to use. Taking notes from bigger names like Audiotree and KEXP-FM, Fuzzy Sweaters approach to recorded live music emphasizes the intimacy and warmth of live music experiences.
