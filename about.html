About Fuzzy Sweater

Video, Photography, Recording, Mixing

Fuzzy Sweater is a production (company?) made up of a couple local artists and their friends, working out of a basement in the hilly Manayunk section of Philadelphia, PA. Prior to Covid-19, the basement operated as a DIY music venue for touring and local bands. As it became unfortunately clear that live music was still a far away dream, Shane & Matt decided to take this as an opportunity to put their audio/video skills to use. Taking notes from bigger names like Audiotree and KEXP-FM, Fuzzy Sweaters approach to recorded live music emphasizes the intimacy and warmth of a live basement show experience.

The goal for us is to provide FREE content for local musicians and artists to share with their fans in hopes that we can help do our part to keep the local music thriving. We are still in the process of getting our stuff together, but we are trying to also provide a revenue stream for both ourselves AND the artists we feature through our various outlets.

More Than Live Performances

We started Fuzzy Sweater in order to compensate for the lack of live shows but we are out here to provide more than just that for the local DIY scene. Between our current team we have the ability to help you with stuff like band photo shoots, music videos, album recording, and tons of other stuff. Currently, we are doing this a service to the community while working full time jobs SO we don't go out of our way to advertise it but please feel free to send us a message at fuzzysweaterproductions@gmail.com with any and all questions regarding how we can help you, your band, or business!!!

We do other stuff too!

While our main project is live sessions, we offer photography and recording/mixing services as well! Please shoot us a message if you'd like to know more.

Who we are


Shane - Audio & Web Design

Fuzzy Sweater co-founder and owner Shane has worked with local artists for years. Only taking a short break to get a B.S. in Computer Science from Bloomsburg University, he has been involved in the local scene for over a decade. Spending his days as a software developer, he spends the rest of his time writing, recording & mixing music for others as well as his own projects.

Matt - Video & Photography

Fuzzy Sweater co-founder and owner Matt has experience with almost any audio or visual art that you can think of. He graduated from West Chester University with a degree in Fine Arts and has been working in the DIY Philly scene for years. Although a multi-instrumentalist, Matt is primarily a drummer for a number of Philly acts.
