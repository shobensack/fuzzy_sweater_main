Recording Sessions

In an effort to help local musicians effectively reach their audiences, starting January 2022, we will be hosting affordable recording sessions at our home studio in Pottstown, PA. Please refer to our rates below, and feel free to reach out to find what works best for your music!

Rates

$50 per song (up to 4 tracks): Recommended flat rate reserved for smaller projects like singles or EPs. This includes recording, engineering, mixing and mastering.

$15 per hour: Reserved for larger projects like an album or full band live session. This includes recording, engineering, mixing and mastering.


Policy

  • Studio hours vary; we are flexible but generally most sessions take place on weekends.
  • 2 hour minimum for all sessions.
  • We require a $30 deposit before your session is officially booked (Venmo preferred.)
  • All cancellations / reschedules require a 24 hour notice, or else you will lose your deposit!
  • Payments must be made in full at the end of the session and before any mixes or files will be provided to the artist.
  • Please refer to our about page for information about who we are and what we do!
